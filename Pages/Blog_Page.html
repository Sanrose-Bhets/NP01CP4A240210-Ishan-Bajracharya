Impact of Technology

Technology in eCommerce refers to the use of digital tools,platforms,and systems to facilitate online transactions, sales, and marketing. It allows customers to browse products, make purchases, view order status and access customer support. It has a significant impact on many different aspects of the e-commerce value chain, imncluding operations, customer service, and advertising. Technology plays a role in the field of e-commerce, enabling businesses to operate efficiently, enhance customer experiences, and stay competitive.

Advantages

  • Enhanced communication and connectivity globally.
  • Automation of repetitive tasks increases productivity.
  • Improved healthcare through advanced medical equipment.
  • Access to vast knowledge and resources online.
  • Innovation in industries leading to economic growth.

Disadvantages

  • Over-dependence on technology can reduce critical thinking.
  • Privacy concerns with data breaches and surveillance.
  • Increase in unemployment due to automation.
  • Digital addiction and negative mental health impacts.
  • Environmental impact due to e-waste and energy consumption.
